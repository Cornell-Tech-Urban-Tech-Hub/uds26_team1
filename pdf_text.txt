INSIGHTS
Artist Studios: An "Invisible" BID Asset
What BIDs Are Built to Support
● Storefronts & retail corridors
● Property owners (who pay assessments)
● "Front-facing" businesses (cafés, bars, restaurants)
Where Artist Studios Sit
● Non-retail, light-industrial space (back lots, upper floors)
● Tenants, not property owners
● Low foot-traffic generation
● Not highly visible to casual visitors
BIDs excel at managing the public realm and supporting visible 
commercial activity but… artist studios are a cultural asset that doesn't fit 
the traditional BID toolkit
